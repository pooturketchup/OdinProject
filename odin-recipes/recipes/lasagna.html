Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a baked dish containing layers of boiled lasagna, and usually cheese, a seasoned sauce of tomatoes, and meat or vegatables.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Sausage

Steps

  1. Spread a thin layer of pasta sauce in the bottom of the baking dish
  2. Make a layer of cooked lasagna noodles
  3. Spread an even layer of the ricotta cheese mixture
  4. Spread an even layer of meat sauce
  5. Repeat those layers two times
  6. Top it with a final layer of noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and parmesan cheese
