Burrito

Description

A burrito is wrapped in a flour tortilla and usually contains meat, beans, sourcream, lettuce.

Ingredients

  • Flour tortilla
  • lettuce
  • sourcream
  • meat-beef/pork/chicken
  • beans

Steps

  1. lay tortilla out
  2. place meat and beans first
  3. top with sourcream and lettuce
  4. add any additional items desired
  5. wrap up into a burrito
