PB&J

Description

A PB&J sandwhich is a sandwhich that is composed of bread, peanutbutter, and jelly.

Ingredients

  • sliced bread
  • peanutbutter
  • jelly

Steps

  1. take one slice of bread and spread peanutbutter on it
  2. take the other slice of bread and spread jelly on it
  3. merge the two slices of bread together to from the sandwhich
